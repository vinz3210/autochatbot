AITwitchBot

AI-powered Twitch chat responses

ℹ️
Compatibility Notice
This app works best in Chromium-based browsers like Chrome, Edge, or Brave.
📡 Monitor multiple channels at once
⚡ Set custom AI pre-prompts
🔢 Optional auto-incrementing counters
🔒 Restrict triggers to specific users

AITwitchBot monitors Twitch chat in real-time and uses a local AI model to generate intelligent responses when trigger phrases are detected.

Getting Started

  • Log in with your Twitch account to authorize chat reading & sending
  • Add channels you want to monitor (e.g., your own channel)
  • Create triggers with a phrase and an AI pre-prompt
  • Keep the page open — it runs as long as the tab is active

Trigger Phrases

Set any text as a trigger (e.g., !hi). When someone types that phrase, AITwitchBot processes your pre-prompt and generates an AI response.

Allowed Users

By default, anyone can activate a trigger. To restrict it, enter comma-separated usernames in the Allowed Users field. Only those users will be able to trigger it — useful for preventing spam.

Placeholders

Use these in your AI pre-prompt:

  • {author} — The user who triggered the command.
  • {message} — The user's full message.
  • {count} — Current counter value.
  • {channel} — Current channel name.

Example pre-prompt: Respond to {author} who said "{message}". Be friendly.

Storage

All channels, triggers, and counter values are saved in your browser's local storage. They persist across page refreshes and browser restarts.
